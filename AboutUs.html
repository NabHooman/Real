About Us

Who We Are

At COMFO, we think everyone should have access to high-performance computers and parts. Our specialty is finding, evaluating, and shipping high-quality used PC parts that function like new without the "new" price tag.

Our Mission

Our goal is to make the secondary market for PC parts less risky. Our goal is to offer a carefully chosen range of parts that have undergone thorough durability and quality testing as a dependable alternative for retail purchases.

Our Vision

Making high-end performance an everyday reality
